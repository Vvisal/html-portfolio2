My Top 3 movie of all the time

Dune (2021)

This sci-fi epic follows Paul Atreides, son of Duke Leto Atreides, as his family accepts control of the desert planet Arrakis, the only source of the spice melange, a valuable commodity that extends life and enhances human abilities. Danger awaits them as they face betrayal, the harsh environment, and the Fremen, a race of people who have lived on Arrakis for generations.

The Batman (2022)

Matt Reeves directs Robert Pattinson as Bruce Wayne/Batman in this dark and gritty take on the superhero. The Batman follows Batman in his second year of fighting crime in Gotham City when he uncovers a web of corruption and conspiracy connected to his own family's past.

Top Gun

Maverick (2022) Tom Cruise reprises his role as Pete "Maverick" Mitchell after more than 30 years in this long-awaited sequel. After more than two decades of service as one of the Navy's top aviators, Pete "Maverick" Mitchell is where he belongs, pushing the envelope as a courageous test pilot and dodging the advancement in rank that would ground him. Training a detachment of elite graduates for a dangerous specialized mission that no pilot has ever flown, Maverick encounters Bradley Bradshaw, the son of his late friend and copilot Goose. Facing an uncertain future and confronting the ghosts of his past, Maverick is drawn into a high-stakes confrontation with forces beyond his control.pen_spark

Inside Out 2 (2024)

This long-awaited sequel to the critically-acclaimed Pixar movie follows Riley as she enters her teenage years. Her emotions, Joy, Sadness, Anger, Disgust, and Fear, have to navigate the complex new world of adolescence.pen_spark
